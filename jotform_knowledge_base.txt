## The Customer Journey (How it Works)
1. **Discovery**: Guest browses `/packages` to find a base itinerary that resonates with their soul.
2. **Personalization**: On the package page, the guest selects dates, party size, and specific "Elite Add-ons" (e.g., Hot Air Balloon flights over the Serengeti, Pro Photography Kits, or Private Chef services).
3. **The Inquiry**: The guest submits their details via our secure funnel. No payment is required at this stage. 
4. **Expert Review**: Within 12-24 hours, an Asili Yetu specialist reviews the inquiry, checks real-time lodge availability, and sends a polished PDF Proposal via email.
5. **The Handoff**: Once the itinerary is refined over WhatsApp or Email, we provide a secure link for a deposit to lock in the dates and vehicles.
6. **The Field Experience**: Guests are met at the airport by their assigned guide. Throughout the trip, our HQ monitors the expedition via **Mission Sentinel** telemetry for maximum safety and logistical precision.

## Our Safari Philosophy (Biomes & Intensity)
- **Savannah Mode**: Focuses on the vast plains (Serengeti/Tarangire). Luxury mobile camps, classic game drives, and high-visibility wildlife spotting.
- **Jungle Mode**: Focuses on the lush, high-altitude forests (Ngorongoro Highlands/Gombe). Deep expeditions, walking safaris, and primal landscapes.
- **Difficulty Scale**:
  - **Levels 1-2 (Serene)**: Suitable for all ages. Minimal walking, high-comfort lodges.
  - **Levels 3-4 (Active)**: Early starts, longer drives, and light trekking.
  - **Level 5 (Elite Expedition)**: Kilimanjaro summits or deep bush tracking. Requires physical preparation.

## Key Destination Facts (Concierge Cheat Sheet)
- **Serengeti**: Home to the Great Migration (June–October). Famous for the "Endless Plains".
- **Ngorongoro Crater**: The world's largest inactive volcanic caldera. A "Garden of Eden" where the Big Five are visible in a single afternoon.
- **Tarangire**: The "Land of Giants" (Elephants and Baobab trees). Best visited during the dry season (July–November).
- **Mount Kilimanjaro**: The Roof of Africa (5,895m). We offer multiple routes including Machame and Lemosho.
