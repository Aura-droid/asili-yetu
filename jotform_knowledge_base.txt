## Destinations Context
- **Serengeti National Park**: 14,763 sq km. Best time to visit is June to October for The Great Migration (Wildebeest, Zebra). Famous for Endless Plains and Lions.
- **Ngorongoro Crater**: 260 sq km. Year-round viewing. A volcanic caldera with the densest Big Five population. Easiest place to spot Black Rhinos.
- **Tarangire National Park**: 2,850 sq km. Best July to November. Huge elephant herds and Baobab trees.
- **Mount Kilimanjaro**: Trekking the Roof of Africa. Best Jan-Mar or Aug-Oct.

## Booking Process
1. Users provide their desired Location, Dates, and Party Size via the Hero component or Packages page.
2. Our System (Expert Curation) matches them with the closest Package and builds a daily breakdown.
3. They provide their Email, Name, and Dietary/Addons in the funnel.
4. This becomes a "New Inquiry". The system sends an automated acknowledgment email to the customer.
5. Our Admin contacts them directly over Email or WhatsApp to finalize the pricing and schedule. 

## Addons & Special Requests
- Guests can request Add-ons like "Hot Air Balloon Flight" or "Pro Photography Kit".
- We accommodate all dietary requirements (Vegan, Nut allergies, etc.) if they inform us gracefully during the quote lookup.
